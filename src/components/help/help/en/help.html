Help Module

If you're reading this paragraph, then you're on the Help Module. This is the third tab of the workspace corresponds to a small manual in which we hope to give you the clue for you to enjoy this program, creating your own animations.

On the right side of the workspace, you'll see the table of contents of this manual. To go to a section or particular title, just to click on the item that interests you.

Fig # 29. Interface Module Help

Tip: Whenever you wish to consult the help module, simply press the Ctrl + 3.

Remember that if you have questions or concerns that can not resolve this manual, you seek further help from the project website http://www.maefloresta.com . Here you can interact with other users and the development team Tupi. Together with our community, your contributions, ideas and suggestions we can turn this project into a professional tool that allows free birth of millions of animated art.
