About Tupi

Tupi is:

  • A design and authoring tool for digital artists interested in 2D Animation, offering an interface experience focused on 8-100 years old kids
  • A software application developed in C++ using the Qt library
  • A free software project under constant redesign and evolution, released under the GPL terms (version 3)
  • A community of artists and developers around a software project
  • A space for creation and publishing of free animated content

The name of the project is inspired on the Tupí people, one of the main ethnic groups of brazilian indigenous people, along with the Guarani natives (sharing a strong relationship).

For more information about Tupi please visit: http://www.maefloresta.com
