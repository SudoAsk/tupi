About Tupi

Tupi is:

  • A design and authoring tool for digital artists interested in. 2D Animation, Offering an interface experience Focus on kids 8-100 years old
  • A Software Application Developer in C + + using the Qt library
  • A free software project under constant redesign and evolution, released under the GPL terms (version 3)
  • A community of artists and developers around a software project
  • A space for creation and publishing of free animated content

The name of the project is inspired on the Tupi people , one of the main ethnic groups of Brazilian indigenous people, the Guarani natives Along with (sharing a strong relationship).
