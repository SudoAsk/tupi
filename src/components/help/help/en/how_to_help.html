How to help!

If you want help Tupi become the best free tool for creating 2D animation projects, there are many ways to do it. We tell some of them:

As an animator

  • Tupi installed on your computer and become an expert creating animations
  • Tell your friends about Tupi leaders
  • Create manuals, videos or any content that supports other entertainers who want to learn
  • Create and publish small animation projects in our gallery
  • Report faults you find in the program through our website
  • Enter our forums to answer your questions or help other animators
  • Make a donation to the project. From USD $ 1, any contribution you make will allow us to hire more developers, acquire better equipment and pay the maintenance schedule on our website. Your support will translate into shorter development cycles and improved versions of Tupi

As a developer

  • Download the project source code and study it
  • Using the tool Doxygen , help us to document the software components of Tupi
  • Develops and publishes charts and visual aids that will enable other developers to know the architecture of Tupi more easily and quickly
  • See the bug reports and help us to correct problems reported animators. Create patches with improvements and share them with our development team
  • Check the schedule of activities and helps to implement any new functionality that is pending

As a teacher

  • Invite your students to learn and use Tupi
  • Involves the creation of animations as part of activities in your courses
  • Participate and encourage your students to create small animations that serve as an example for other students
  • Develop tutorials and invite your students to participate in its creation

Independent of the role you choose, all your contributions as a member of this community are part of the process of development and improvement of Tupi. Join the team!
